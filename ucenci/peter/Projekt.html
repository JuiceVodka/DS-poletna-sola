Harry Potter 1
Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone is a 2001 fantasy film. The film stars Daniel Radcliffe as Harry Potter, with Rupert Grint as Ron Weasley, and Emma Watson as Hermione Granger. Its story follows Harry's first year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry as he discovers that he is a famous wizard.

Harry Potter 2
Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets is a 2002 fantasy film. The film stars Daniel Radcliffe as Harry Potter, with Rupert Grint and Emma Watson as his best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. The story follows Harry's second year at Hogwarts School, where the Heir of Salazar Slytherin opens the Chamber of Secrets.

Harry Potter 3
Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban is a 2004 fantasy film. It stars Daniel Radcliffe as Harry Potter, alongside Rupert Grint and Emma Watson as Harry's best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. The film follows Harry's third year at Hogwarts and his quest to uncover the truth about his past.

Harry Potter 4
Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire is a 2005 fantasy film. The film stars Daniel Radcliffe as Harry Potter, alongside Rupert Grint and Emma Watson as Harry's best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger respectively. Its story follows Harry's fourth year at Hogwarts as he is chosen by the Goblet of Fire to compete in the Triwizard Tournament.

Harry Potter 5
Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix is a 2007 fantasy film. The film stars Daniel Radcliffe as Harry Potter, alongside Rupert Grint and Emma Watson as Harry's best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. The story follows Harry's fifth year at Hogwarts School as the Ministry of Magic is scared of Voldemort's return.

Harry Potter 6
Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince is a 2009 fantasy film. It stars Daniel Radcliffe as Harry Potter, alongside Rupert Grint and Emma Watson as Harry's best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger respectively. The story follows Harry's sixth year at Hogwarts as he tries to retrieve the key to Lord Voldemort's downfall.

Harry Potter 7
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 1 is a 2010 fantasy film. It is based on J. K. Rowling's 2007 novel of the same name and the seventh instalment in the Harry Potter film series. It was written by Steve Kloves and produced by David Heyman, David Barron, and Rowling and features an ensemble cast.

Harry Potter 8
Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 2 is a 2011 fantasy film. It is the second of two cinematic parts based on J. K. Rowling's 2007 novel of the same name and the eighth and final instalment in the Harry Potter film series. It was written by Steve Kloves and produced by David Heyman, David Barron, and Rowling.
